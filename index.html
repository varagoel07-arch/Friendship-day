Dear Bestie,

with love, from your best friend

I've been trying to write this for a while, and I keep stopping because I don't know how to fit everything into words. But here goes.

You are the person I call when everything falls apart and when everything is great. You're the one who shows up — not just when it's convenient, but when it actually counts. You're the only one who gets my weird references, who laughs at the same things, who knows exactly when to push back and when to just be there.

Thank you for being a reliable person in my corner. For the late-night plans, the random calls, and every time you pretended not to notice I was a mess.

I hope today feels like yours. I hope someone gets the order right, the wifi doesn't drop, and you laugh at least once for no reason. And I hope you know — even when I don't say it — I'm so glad you're my best friend.

💗 with love (and zero regrets), — Your Best Friend
